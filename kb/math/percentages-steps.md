# Percentages: Word Problems & Step Hints
**Steps to solve**
1) Read the question. What % ? Of what number?
2) Change % to fraction over 100.
3) Multiply by the number.

**Examples**
- 20% of 50 = (20/100)*50 = 10
- 15% of 200 = (15/100)*200 = 30

**Practice**
- 30% of 70 = ?
- 5% of 120 = ?
- 12% of 50 = ?

**Tiny Hints**
- 10% is easy: divide by 10.
- 5% = half of 10%.
- 1% = divide by 100, then scale up.

**Flashcards**
Q: How to get 5% quickly?  
A: Find 10%, then half it.  

Q: 1% of 400?  
A: 4.
