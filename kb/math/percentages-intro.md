# Percentages: Easy Start (Grades 3–5)
Percent means "per 100". 20% means 20 out of 100.

**Key ideas**
- 50% = half
- 25% = quarter
- 10% = one tenth
- 1% = one out of 100

**How to find a percentage of a number**
- 10% of a number = divide by 10
- 5% = first 10%, then half it
- 1% = divide by 100

**Examples**
- 10% of 80 = 8
- 25% of 40 = 10
- 1% of 300 = 3

**Quick Hints**
- Look for 10%, 5%, 1% steps.
- 50% is the same as dividing by 2.

**Practice (short)**
- 10% of 60 = ?
- 25% of 32 = ?
- 1% of 900 = ?
- 50% of 18 = ?
- 5% of 40 = ?

**Flashcards**
Q: What does 20% mean?  
A: 20 out of 100.  

Q: 50% of 14?  
A: 7.  

Q: 10% of 90?  
A: 9.  

**Puzzles/Games**
- “Shopping Sale”: If a toy is 100 Rs and there is 10% off, how much is the discount?
- “Fill to 100”: You have 30%. How much more to reach 100%?
